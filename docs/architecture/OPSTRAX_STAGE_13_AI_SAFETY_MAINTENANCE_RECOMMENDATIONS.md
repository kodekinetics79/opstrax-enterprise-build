# Opstrax Stage 13 AI Safety and Maintenance Recommendations

## Safety Rules

- AI may recommend review, coaching, or maintenance attention.
- AI may not close incidents or complete work orders directly.
- AI may not bypass tenant RBAC or dashboard permissions.

## Maintenance Rules

- AI can suggest a work order or PM review.
- AI cannot mark a defect resolved or a vehicle available.
- AI cannot replace the live maintenance dashboard.

## Operational Stance

- Any recommendation shown in Stage 13 must be clearly labeled as assistive.
- The UI must continue to show the underlying live record state.
