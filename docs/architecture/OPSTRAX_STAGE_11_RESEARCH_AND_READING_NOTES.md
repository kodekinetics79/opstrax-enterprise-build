# Opstrax Stage 11 Research and Reading Notes

This document records the files and areas reviewed before the Stage 11 build slice was chosen.

| Area | Files/Docs Read | Key Finding | Risk | Stage 11 Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Stage 10 completion | `docs/architecture/OPSTRAX_STAGE_10_COMPLETION_REPORT.md`, `docs/architecture/OPSTRAX_STAGE_10_DELIVERY_ASSURANCE_REVIEW.md` | Stage 10 is genuinely complete locally, with the operational proof center and mobile-safe API contracts already in place. | Low | Do not rebuild Stage 10. Stage 11 must add a new enterprise slice. |
| Platform admin backend | `backend-dotnet/Controllers/PlatformEndpoints.cs`, `backend-dotnet/Services/PlatformSchemaService.cs`, `backend-dotnet/Program.cs` | Platform auth, sessions, RBAC, tenants, packages, billing, health, audit, and roles are already wired end-to-end. | Medium | Treat platform admin as a real subsystem that can be refined into a stronger commercial cockpit. |
| Platform admin frontend | `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformApp.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformCommandCenterPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformTenantsPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformPackagesPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformRevenuePage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformBillingPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformHealthPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/platform/PlatformAuditPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/layouts/PlatformShell.tsx` | The platform surface already has a separate auth context, navigation, and operational pages. | Medium | Build on top of this shell instead of inventing a new admin stack. |
| Tenant governance | `frontend/src/pages/AdminPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/services/adminApi.ts`, `frontend/src/hooks/useAdmin.ts`, `backend-dotnet/Controllers/EndpointMappings.cs` | Users, roles, permissions, audit export, and settings already exist in a working governance module. | Medium | Do not re-implement governance from scratch in Stage 11. |
| Telemetry / IoT | `backend-dotnet/Services/TelemetrySchemaService.cs`, `backend-dotnet/Services/TelemetryBackgroundService.cs`, `frontend/src/pages/LiveMapPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/IotDevicesPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/TelematicsCommandPage.tsx` | Telemetry is broad and real, but it is already a large operational domain with many moving parts. | High | Defer unless it is the chosen product priority; it is too large for a quick completion slice. |
| CRM / sales | `frontend/src/pages/LeadsPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/OpportunitiesPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/CustomersPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/ContractsPage.tsx`, `frontend/src/pages/QuotationsPage.tsx`, `backend-dotnet/Services/RevenueReadinessService.cs`, `backend-dotnet/Services/RevenueSchemaService.cs` | CRM and commercial pages exist, but the surface is spread across multiple modules and not the cleanest single-slice completion target. | Medium | Do not expand CRM without a sharper module boundary. |
| Customer portal | `frontend/src/pages/CustomerVisibilityPage.tsx`, `backend-dotnet/Controllers/EndpointMappings.cs`, `backend-dotnet/Services/CustomerVisibilitySchemaService.cs` | Customer visibility is valuable, but it is more customer-facing than SaaS-control-plane oriented. | Medium | Keep it as a later product slice unless buyer demand changes priority. |
| Local safety | `git status --short`, `git diff --stat`, `git diff --check` | The worktree already contains earlier-stage work, but there are no whitespace issues from the baseline shell check. | Low | Keep the Stage 11 diff narrow and additive. |
| Decision | This stage should complete one enterprise cockpit instead of starting another broad vertical. | Current repo maturity and user request | High | Choose the platform commercial operations slice as the best bounded, high-leverage Stage 11 build. |
